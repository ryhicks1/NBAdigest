FAQ

What is BetStreak.ai?

BetStreak.ai is an AI-powered NBA anomaly detection tool. It identifies players and teams on statistically significant hot or cold streaks by comparing recent performance against season and last-10-game averages, then surfaces potential value against current betting lines.

How does anomaly detection work?

We compare each player's last 3 games (L3) against their season average and last 10 games (L10) average. When the L3 deviates significantly from these baselines, we flag it as an anomaly. Hot streaks indicate a player is performing well above their norm; cold streaks indicate the opposite. These deviations are then cross-referenced with live Sportsbet betting lines to highlight potential value.

What data sources does BetStreak.ai use?

Player and team statistics are sourced directly from NBA.com's official stats API. Betting lines and odds are pulled from Sportsbet.com.au. Data is refreshed daily before game tip-offs.

Yes, BetStreak.ai is completely free to use. If you find it useful, you can optionally support the project via the link in the footer.
